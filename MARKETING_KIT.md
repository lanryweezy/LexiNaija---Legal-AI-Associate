# How LexiNaija 1.0.0 Solves Three Major Pain Points for Nigerian Lawyers

Being a lawyer in Nigeria is demanding. Between court appearances, client meetings, and endless paperwork, it's easy to let critical deadlines slip or undercharge for your services.

We heard you. And today, we're launching three features that directly address the biggest pain points we've heard from hundreds of Nigerian lawyers.

## 1. S.84 Certificates: From 45 Minutes to 30 Seconds

### The Problem
Section 84 of the Evidence Act 2011 requires certificates for electronic evidence. Manually drafting these takes 30-45 minutes per document, with room for typos that judges reject.

### The Solution
Our S.84 Certificate Generator:
- Automatically fingerprints your device
- Generates SHA-256 integrity hash
- Produces court-ready certificates in 30 seconds
- Downloads instantly for your convenience

### The Impact
One lawyer told us: *"I have 15 exhibits in a fraud case. That's 11 hours of S.84 work. LexiNaija just gave me my day back."*

## 2. LPRO 2023: Never Undercharge Again

### The Problem
The Legal Practitioners (Remuneration) Order 2023 sets minimum fees. But calculating statutory fees is complex - and many lawyers accidentally undercharge (or worse, violate the Order).

### The Solution
Our LPRO Calculator:
- Handles Sale, Lease, Mortgage, Assignment
- Applies correct tiers (10%/5%/3% for sales)
- Includes VAT, Stamp Duty, Registration
- Warns if you're below statutory minimum

### The Impact
On a ₦100M property sale:
- Statutory fee: ₦10M
- Typical charge: ₦3M
- **Recovered revenue: ₦7M**

One calculation pays for 12 months of subscription.

## 3. Limitation Kill-Switch: Prevent Malpractice

### The Problem
Missing a limitation deadline is career-ending. But tracking multiple cases with different limitation periods (3 years for tort, 6 for contract, 1 for fundamental rights) is overwhelming.

### The Solution
Our Limitation Kill-Switch:
- Tracks all your cases' limitation dates
- Alerts at 90, 60, 30 days
- Highlights critical cases (<30 days)
- Shows on your dashboard daily

### The Impact
One prevented statute-bar = one career saved.
